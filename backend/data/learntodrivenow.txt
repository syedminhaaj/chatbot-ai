Learn To Drive Now — Website Content Summary
Overview

Learn To Drive Now is a premier driving instruction program located in Oshawa, Ontario, serving the Durham Region. It provides MTO-approved Beginner Driver Education (BDE) courses to help students earn their G or G2 licence with confidence and skill. 
Learn To Drive Now

Their instructors are experienced (18+ years), fully licensed, insured, and Ontario Safety League certified. The program emphasizes safety, confidence, and real-world driving skills.

G1 Restrictions:

Zero blood alcohol

Passenger seatbelts required

No driving between 12 a.m.–5 a.m.

Accompanied by a licensed driver with 4+ years experience (unless with a certified instructor). 
Learn To Drive Now

G2 Rules:

Can drive alone with zero blood alcohol

Passenger restrictions for drivers under 19 for the first 6 months. 
Learn To Drive Now

Maximum In-Vehicle Training:
No more than 2 hours per day. After 5 hours of classroom, you may not do more than 1 hour of in-car training that day. 
Learn To Drive Now

Course Start:
You can begin classroom/online learning before having a G1; in-car lessons only after obtaining G1. BDE courses must be completed within 1 year. 
Learn To Drive Now

Certification:
Certification is issued online (pursuant to Ontario MTO standards). To obtain paper proof, you must retrieve it via ServiceOntario. 
Learn To Drive Now
